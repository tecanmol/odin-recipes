Litti Chokha

Description

Litti is a whole wheat flour dough ball that is stuffed with an earthy, spiced mixture of sattu or roasted black chickpea or kala chana flour. Chokha, on the other hand, is a very basic mashed relish made of vegetables like brinjal (aubergine/eggplant), potato, tomato, etc

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Lemon juice
  • Baking soda (optional)
  • Sattu (roasted gram flour)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Coriander leaves
  • Carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Fennel seeds
  • Mustard oil
  • Brinjal (eggplant)
  • Tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Nigella seeds (kalonji)
  • Cumin seeds
  • Asafoetida (hing)

Steps

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, and ghee. Add water and knead to make a dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix sattu, onion, garlic, ginger, green chilies, coriander leaves, carom seeds, fennel seeds, salt, mustard oil, and lemon juice for the filling.
  3. Divide dough into balls, stuff with filling, and seal.
  4. Bake at 200°C (392°F) for 20-25 minutes or cook on a grill, turning occasionally.
  5. Dip in melted ghee before serving.
  6. Roast brinjal, tomatoes, and potatoes until charred and soft. Peel and mash.
  7. Add chopped onion, garlic, green chilies, coriander leaves, mustard oil, salt, and red chili powder. Mix well.
